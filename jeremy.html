Jeremy Darrow

I am a passionate pianist that loves to play classical music. I am thirteen years old and I live currently in San Jose, California. I want share my talent with the world and broaden my repertoire of classical piano pieces.


My Story

I have been playing piano for over 2 years. My love for piano started when my dad said that we were going to sell the piano if no one was going to play it. It was not that I forced myself to play the piano because of that. It's just that like that, in just a snap, I suddenly found a desire. It was something that filled my life with a hobby, or a activity that I found interest in.
